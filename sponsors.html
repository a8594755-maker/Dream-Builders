Our Sponsors

Recognizing our partners who make this mission possible

Primary Partner

Sleep in Heavenly Peace – New River Valley (SHP–NRV)

Our primary partner organization dedicated to ensuring every child has a bed to sleep in. SHP–NRV leads the build efforts and coordinates with families in need throughout the New River Valley area.
