Story

Why This Project Matters

Our team is combining outreach, donations, and hands-on support to help local children receive safe and comfortable sleeping spaces.

Dream Builders partners with Sleep in Heavenly Peace (NRV) to remove a basic barrier to healthy childhood development: not having a bed. We focus on coordinated action that keeps effort practical and measurable.

Our current phase emphasizes three priorities: building support through fundraising, collecting twin bed-in-a-bag sets, and aligning volunteers for key April build activities.

Current Priorities

  • Grow community fundraising contributions.
  • Collect 40 twin bedding sets by April 25.
  • Support observation and main build-day execution.
  • Maintain clear timelines, roles, and communication.
