Bedding Drive Plan

Strategy for collecting 40 twin bed-in-a-bag sets by April 25, 2026

Executive Summary

This bedding drive plan outlines a comprehensive strategy to collect 40 twin bed-in-a-bag sets for children receiving beds through the Sleep in Heavenly Peace (NRV) program. The drive will run from February 16 through April 20, 2026, with multiple collection points, community partnerships, and organized sorting and distribution processes.

Sorting and Quality Control Process

Step 1: Collection Intake

Each donation receives a receipt with tracking number. Donor information recorded for thank-you notes and impact reporting.

Step 2: Initial Quality Check

Quick assessment for obvious issues (stains, tears, wrong size). Items not meeting standards are politely declined with explanation.

Step 3: Detailed Inspection

Thorough inspection for quality, completeness, and age-appropriateness. Items sorted by theme, color, and target age group.

Step 4: Inventory Recording

Each accepted set photographed, inventoried, and entered into tracking system. Progress updated on public dashboard.

Step 5: Storage Preparation

Sets cleaned (if needed), folded, and stored in climate-controlled environment. Organized by theme and age group.

Step 6: Distribution Planning

Sets matched with specific children based on age, gender, and preferences. Prepared for distribution with bed delivery.
